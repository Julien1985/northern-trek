Faqs

Have questions on your mind?

  • 01 What is Northern Trek?
    Northern Trek is an annual endurance fundraiser founded in 2018 to raise funds and awareness for the Canadian Fallen Firefighters Foundation. Through physically demanding personal challenges, it honours fallen firefighters while supporting their families across Canada.
  • 02 How does Northern Trek support firefighters and their families?
    All donations raised through Northern Trek go directly to the Canadian Fallen Firefighters Foundation, helping provide support services to families, peers, and fire departments affected by line-of-duty deaths.
  • 03 What is the Northern Trek 2026 challenge?
    The 2026 Northern Trek will be a six-day, self-sustained kayak expedition through the Wapiti, Smoky, and Peace Rivers in northern Alberta, completed without external support crews.
  • 04 Who benefits from funds raised through Northern Trek?
    Funds support career, volunteer, industrial, military, and wildland firefighters, as well as the families and departments impacted by firefighter line-of-duty deaths across Canada.
  • 05 How can I support Northern Trek?
    Support can be provided through sponsorship, direct donations, or by helping share awareness of the fundraiser and its mission across communities and organizations.
